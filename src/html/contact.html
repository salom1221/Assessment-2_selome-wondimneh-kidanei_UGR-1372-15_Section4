Our Designers

  • Luna Blackthorn

    • Luna is a visionary designer inspired by the mysticism of the moon and the haunting beauty of gothic art. Her designs often merge delicate lacework with creating pieces that exude mystery and elegance.


  • Damien Ashcroft

    • Damien brings rebellious energy to Freya Clothing. Drawing influence from 90s grunge and Y2K streetwear, his designs are a perfect mix of distressed textures, and oversized silhouettes.


  • Ivy Ravenshade

    • Ivy is the embodiment of dark academia, blending classic tailoring with modern minimalism. Her designs are inspired by old libraries, and the timeless appeal of vintage fashion.

About

At Freya Clothing, we believe in empowering individuality through fashion. Our brand is dedicated to creating stylish, sustainable, and high-quality apparel for all. Inspired by the beauty of everyday life, our collections blend contemporary designs with timeless elegance. We are committed to ethical practices and strive to make a positive impact on the environment and communities. Join us on our journey to celebrate self-expression and embrace your unique style with Freya Clothing.
